Multi-Modal Fusion

Combines NIAGADS genetic variants (6,346 SNP records) with brain MRI features for comprehensive risk assessment.

Cross-Modal Attention

Learns interactions between genetic variants and brain regions with interpretable attention weights.

Multi-Task Learning

Simultaneous disease stage classification and progression risk prediction.

Clinical Utility

Generates patient-specific risk reports with confidence scores for clinical decision support.
